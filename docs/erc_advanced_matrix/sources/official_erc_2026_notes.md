## Facts Captured On 2026-06-27

- ERC Advanced Grants support established, leading Principal Investigators.
- Applications are bottom-up and can be made in any field of research.
- Research must be hosted by a public or private research organisation in an EU
  Member State or associated country.
- Advanced Grants may be awarded up to EUR 2.5M for up to 5 years.
- Additional funding may be requested for eligible major costs; the ERC page
  notes EUR 1M in ordinary cases and up to EUR 2M for PIs moving from
  non-associated third countries to the EU or an associated country.
- ERC grants can cover 100 percent of eligible direct costs plus a 25 percent
  contribution to indirect costs.
- The 2026 Advanced Grant call is a lump-sum pilot.
- The official ERC page lists publication date 28 May 2026 and deadline
  27 August 2026.
- Evaluation is based on excellence as the sole criterion, applied to both the
  research project and the Principal Investigator.
